Hi, I'm Yunus, a fourth-year math PhD student.

Between 11am-1pm on Wednesdays and Sundays, I offer free private tutoring to all of the UW-Madison student body.

I welcome every student, wherever they are at their education. In the past, my students ranged from first-year college students to PhD students. I also welcome any subject where I think I can contribute. Students generally only want my help in math, but I have also tutored computer science, physics, finance and statistics!

If you're interested, please fill out the form and indicate when you would like to meet me this week. I will send you an email the night before and let you know if I can offer you a spot for this week. Please fill out the form by 5pm of the day before you want the tutoring. Thanks!

The Wednesday sessions are in person, and the Sunday sessions are over Zoom.

Please feel free to send me an email if you have any questions.

Subjects tutored:

  • Mathematics (linear algebra, differential equations, calculus 1, 2 and 3, proof writing, statistics, discrete math)
  • Computer Science (algorithms, complexity theory)
  • Finance (corporate finance)
  • Economics (micro economics, econometrics)
